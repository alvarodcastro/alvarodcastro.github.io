This paper studied a smart parking CPS built around a CAN fieldbus and an IP-connected ANPR subsystem. We defined attack scenarios on the CAN bus and on the ANPR path, and related them to concrete effects on availability, safety, and access control. We then present a passive CAN IDS which provides useful protection against relevant attacks in legacy fieldbus deployments
