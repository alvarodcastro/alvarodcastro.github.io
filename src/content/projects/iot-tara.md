## Research Objective

Reduce the manual effort and inconsistency in threat modelling for IoT deployments by automating asset enumeration, threat mapping, and risk scoring.

## Results

The toolset was validated against three industrial IoT case studies. Results contributed to a published journal paper on IoT risk management.
