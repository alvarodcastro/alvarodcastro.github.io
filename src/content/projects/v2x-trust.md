## Research Objective

Design and evaluate trust mechanisms that enable vehicles in a cooperative ITS environment to identify and exclude misbehaving nodes without a centralised authority.

## Methodology

Simulation-driven research using SUMO + Veins (OMNeT++) with realistic mobility traces. Threat scenarios include position falsification, sudden-stop attacks, and Sybil node injection.

## Defensive Value

Improving the robustness of V2X cooperative awareness against adversarial manipulation, directly relevant to automated driving safety systems.
